About the Event

Welcome to Toni’s Chess Nights
Monthly casual tournaments hosted in the cozy atmosphere of Toni’s Pizza. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just learning the game, everyone is welcome to compete, connect, and enjoy.

These events aren’t just for fun
Players also earn ranking points that contribute to their overall standings in the Mate Tournaments system. It's your chance to sharpen your skills, meet fellow chess enthusiasts, and maybe even grab a slice of victory along with your pizza.

Format & Rules

  • Each tournament follows a casual knockout system with quick, enjoyable games suited for all skill levels
  • Players earn ranking points that count toward the season finale
  • Rapid time control

Entry & Fees

  • R150 per player grants tournament entry
  • Prizes and vouchers sponsored by the restaurant add extra excitement to every event
  • Reserve your spot easily online or sign up on the night

Prizes & Rewards

  • R600 in Toni Vouchers
  • The winner plays free on the next event
