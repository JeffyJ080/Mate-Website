About The Knight Circuit

A league type tournament whith a floating trophy for the top campus. You'll have two chances a year to take back this trophy or to defend your campus's honor.

Format & Structure

  • Up to 10 players per university
  • Swiss system (team-based)
  • Rapid time control
  • 2 tournaments

Entry & Fees

  • R200 per player, per tournament
  • Institutional registration

Awards

  • Floating trophy for top university
  • Prizes for winning teams

Eligibility

  • Closed to invite only universities
  • Teams will be picked by the university

For more info don't hesitate to contact us
