Moot Mat Inter-School Chess League

The Moot Mat League is an exclusive inter-school chess series for top-performing primary and high schools in the Moot area. Schools compete across multiple tournaments throughout the year in a team-based format that encourages strategy, pride, and excellence.

Organized by Mate Tournaments in partnership with Yes4Chess, Moot Mat delivers a competitive yet community-driven chess experience — ending in a grand slam finale to crown the best chess school in the region.

When the final piece falls,
only one school will stand victorious.
Every move counts. Every match matters.
